Our Story

Bringing joy to homes, one beautifully crafted piece at a time

Welcome to Joy in A Box

Founded in 2020 with a simple belief: your home should be a reflection of your unique story. We curate and create modern home décor that blends timeless elegance with contemporary design.

Every piece in our collection is thoughtfully selected or custom-crafted to bring warmth, beauty, and functionality to your space. From our signature made-to-order furniture to our cozy Fluff & Snuggles textiles, we're committed to quality that lasts.

We work directly with artisans and small studios around the world, ensuring ethical production practices and sustainable materials. When you shop with us, you're not just decorating your home — you're supporting craftsmanship and creativity.

Our Values

Quality Craftsmanship

We believe in pieces that stand the test of time, using premium materials and skilled artistry.

Joy in Every Box
